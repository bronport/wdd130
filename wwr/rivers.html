Rivers & Trips

Dry Oar offers rafting adventures for all experience levels. Whether you’re looking for calm scenic floats or thrilling whitewater rapids, our guided trips are designed to help you enjoy the outdoors safely while creating unforgettable memories.

Trip Description

Our river trips combine excitement, safety, and stunning scenery. Experienced guides lead you through exciting rapids while also allowing time to relax, swim, and take in the natural beauty around you.

Trips range from single-day adventures to multi-day experiences that include camping, meals, and all necessary rafting equipment.

What to Bring

  • Water-safe shoes or sandals
  • Sunscreen and sunglasses
  • Quick-dry clothing
  • Reusable water bottle
  • A positive attitude and sense of adventure
